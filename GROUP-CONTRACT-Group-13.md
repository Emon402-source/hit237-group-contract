# GROUP-CONTRACT-Individual

## Project Theme
This project will initially be completed individually. In future assessments, the work will transition into a group-based project using an approved theme from Learnline.

---

## Section 1: Terms and Conditions of Work

### Work Responsibility
At this stage, all tasks will be completed individually. However, I will follow a structured approach that can be adapted to group collaboration in future assessments.

### Communication
Currently, progress will be tracked using GitHub commits and personal notes. In future group work, communication tools such as Microsoft Teams and GitHub will be used for collaboration.

### Task Management
All tasks will be planned and completed independently. Tasks will be broken down into smaller parts to ensure steady progress. This structure will later help in dividing tasks among group members.

### Time Management
I will follow a weekly schedule and aim to complete tasks before deadlines. This habit will support effective coordination in future group work.

### Problem Handling
Any issues will first be resolved independently through research. If needed, I will seek guidance from the lecturer. In future group settings, issues will be discussed collaboratively.

### Academic Integrity
All work will be original. Any use of external resources or AI tools will be properly understood and used responsibly. This approach will also be maintained in future group work.

## Section 2: Provisional Milestones and Checkpoints

### Milestones
- Week 1–2: Planning and research
- Week 3–5: Initial development
- Week 6–8: Further development and improvements
- Week 9: Integration and testing
- Week 10: Final review and submission

### Task Breakdown
- Planning: Define scope and requirements
- Development: Implement features step by step
- Documentation: Maintain structured records
- Testing: Identify and fix issues

### Checkpoints
Weekly self-review will be conducted to monitor progress. This will later be expanded into group review meetings.

### Integration
All work will be continuously updated in GitHub. In future group work, contributions will be merged collaboratively.
